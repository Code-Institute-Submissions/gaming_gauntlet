Snake

Eat 25 pieces of fruit to win! Run out of lives by hitting the walls or your own tail 2 times and its game over...

The higher your current multiplier the faster your snake moves!

Use the directional arrow keys on desktop or the four arrow buttons on mobile to control the paddle

BREAKOUT

Break all but 4 blocks in one life to win! run out of lives and its game over...

The higher your current multiplier the faster the ball moves!

Use the left and right arrow keys on desktop or the two arrow buttons on mobile to control the paddle

Guess The Number

Guess the number using hints provided with each life used. Lose all your lives and its game over and your multiplier resets...

The higher your current multiplier the bigger the range of possible numbers is!

Type your answer into the space provided followed by enter to check your guess

Quiz

The higher your multiplier the more difficult the questions are

the first three difficulties are all about gameing while the last two are computer science based!
